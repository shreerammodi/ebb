# ebb user guide

## What is ebb

> TODO: one-paragraph pitch. Local-first, privacy-centric, keyboard-first web
> app for flowing competitive debate rounds. No backend; all data in the
> browser.

## Flowing

> TODO: each column is a speech; type an argument in a cell. Enter commits and
> drops down so responses stack beneath it; Tab commits and moves right to
> answer across into the next speech.

## Sheets and sides

> TODO: a sheet is one flow. Aff reads blue, neg red (recolor under Settings >
> Display). Add sheets with the shortcuts, reorder in the sidebar, jump to one
> by its number. Switch speeches from the header to land on any speech's top
> row across every sheet.

## Split view

> TODO: lay two sheets side by side, like reading a DA next to case on paper.
> Move focus between panes with focus-left / focus-right; picking a sheet in
> the sidebar or palette retargets the focused pane.

## Marking cells

> TODO: bold a claim, highlight what matters, tag a cell as a card (evidence).
> Select a run of cells and group them to bracket a cluster of responses under
> one argument. These combine freely and surface in search.

## Command palette

> TODO: runs every action by name when a shortcut slips your mind.

## Find anything

> TODO: search cells to jump to any argument across every sheet.

## Round info

> TODO: record teams, judge, and decision for scouting later. Paste a Tabroom
> pairing to autofill the sheet; if it already has details, it asks before
> replacing them.

## Writing an RFD

> TODO: open the RFD drawer to write your decision while scanning the flow. On
> a ">" blockquote line, start typing and pick a suggestion to drop the exact
> wording of any cell into your reasoning. Toggle Preview to render it; enable
> vim keybindings in Settings > Display.

## Settings

> TODO: Display (theme, flow font, argument colors, RFD editor), Keyboard
> (rebind any shortcut), Updates (desktop only). Point out that settings are
> meant to be explored.

## Your data

> TODO: everything lives in this browser and autosaves as you type. Nothing is
> sent anywhere; undo reaches back through your whole history.

## Config file (desktop)

> TODO: on the desktop app, settings mirror to a plain-text config.toml (in
> $XDG_CONFIG_HOME/ebb, else ~/.config/ebb, or %APPDATA%\ebb on Windows). Edit
> it in any editor and changes apply live; app changes write back, and comments
> you add are kept.
